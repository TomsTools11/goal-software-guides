# **GOAL Competition Research**

# **1\. Market Overview**

The insurance lead generation market connects consumers shopping for insurance with agents and carriers. This market has evolved from phone-based referral networks to sophisticated digital platforms leveraging programmatic advertising, real-time bidding, and AI-driven optimization.

## **How the Traditional Model Works**

In the traditional model, lead generation companies (aggregators) run digital advertising campaigns to attract consumers shopping for insurance. These consumers fill out quote request forms. The aggregator then sells these leads to multiple insurance agents simultaneously — typically 4 to 8 agents per lead. This creates intense competition among agents calling the same consumer, leading to low contact rates, frustrated consumers, and poor conversion.

## **Where GOAL Fits**

GOAL Platform disrupts this model by putting the agent in control. Instead of buying shared leads from aggregators, agents use GOAL to run their own consumer acquisition campaigns through custom-branded QuoteFunnels. This means the leads are exclusive to the agent from the start — no sharing, no competing, and significantly higher conversion rates.

## **Key Market Segments**

| Segment | How It Works | Key Players |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Lead Aggregators / Marketplaces** | Run ads, collect consumer info, sell leads to multiple agents. Volume-driven. | EverQuote, QuoteWizard (LendingTree), SmartFinancial, NetQuote |
| **Programmatic Exchanges** | Real-time bidding marketplace connecting advertisers with publishers. Agents bid for clicks/leads at auction. | MediaAlpha |
| **Self-Serve Bidding Platforms** | Agents set their own bids, filters, and budgets to buy leads directly. More control than aggregators. | NextGen Leads, ZipQuote |
| **Live Transfer / Call Providers** | Pre-qualify consumers by phone and transfer live calls to agents. Highest intent, highest cost. | Datalot/Centerfield, SmartFinancial (calls) |
| **Agent-Owned Acquisition (GOAL)** | Agents generate their OWN exclusive leads via branded funnels, pay-per-click model, full ownership. | GOAL Platform |

GOAL occupies a fundamentally different position from all other competitors. While every other player in this market is a middleman between the consumer and the agent, GOAL empowers the agent to become their own lead generation engine.
